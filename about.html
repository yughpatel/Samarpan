Our Story

Samarpan, the Gujarati Cultural Association of Kuwait, was established over 15 years ago with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a home away from home for Gujaratis living in Kuwait. What began as small gatherings of families has grown into a thriving community of over 2,500 members.

Under the patronage of the Indian Embassy in Kuwait, Samarpan has become a cultural beacon that preserves our rich Gujarati heritage while embracing our life in Kuwait. We celebrate our festivals, speak our language, cook our traditional food, and dance to our folk music, all while building a supportive family network.

From organizing grand Diwali celebrations to hosting educational workshops for children, from Garba nights that light up Kuwait to community service initiatives, Samarpan has touched thousands of lives.
