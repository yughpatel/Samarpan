About Samarpan

A beacon of Gujarati culture in Kuwait

Samarpan, the Gujarati Cultural Association of Kuwait, operates under the aegis of the Indian Embassy and serves as a vital community platform for Gujaratis residing in Kuwait. Since our establishment, we have been dedicated to preserving and promoting Gujarati culture, traditions, and values.

Our mission is to create a vibrant community where Gujarati families can celebrate their heritage, participate in cultural events, support one another, and pass down our rich traditions to the next generation. Through festivals, social initiatives, and cultural programs, we strengthen the bonds within our community.
